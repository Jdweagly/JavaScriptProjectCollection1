Halo Timeline

Read the full timeline on Halofanon!
  • 2525 Covenant Space — High Charity: Notification of the recent findings of the Minor Transgression are successfully transmitted to the Ministry of Tranquility. Vice Minister of Tranquility Lod Mron intercepts the report and, realizing its implications, dispatches the Covenant light cruiser Rapid Conversion to the Epsilon Indi system. Notably, the Rapid Conversion is the only cruiser in the Covenant Navy commanded and crewed by Jiralhanae. Epsilon Indi system: Human and Covenant forces engage in a brief skirmish aboard the human freighter Bulk Discount, resulting in the destruction of the Covenant missionary vessel Minor Transgression. Two Covenant personnel escape the vessel's destruction, however.
  • 2527 Covenant space: Covenant fleet Strong Condemnation departs for the human colony of Ridgeway's Folly, under the command of Fleet Master Lav 'Utaralee. The fleet comprises five warships: 1 cruiser, 1 destroyer, and 3 corvettes.
  • 2530Groombridge 1830 system: UNSC forces engage and destroy a small Covenant flotilla.
  • 2531 Epsilon Indi system: The Covenant Fleet of Glorious Interdiction departs for resupply; at least two cruisers are dispatched to investigate a forerunner artifact site.
  • 2535 Lambda Serpentis system: Jericho VII is evacuated as UNSC space forces withdraw; Covenant forces subsequently glass the planet.
  • 2537 New Harmony: Covenant forces engage UNSC defenses and successfully land ground forces on the colony.
  • 2545 Draco system - Draco III: Covenant forces assail the colony world and land troops on the surface.
  • 2549 UEG Space: The LOMI Conspiracy is illegally initiated aboard the stealth cruiser UNSC Lord of Monster Island.
  • 2550 Lusitania: Covenant forces approach Lusitania and are engaged by the Lusitania Defense Fleet; local and regional UNSC battle groups are informed of the attack and begin moving towards Lusitania to reinforce the inner colony's defense forces.
  • 2552 Ariel: Covenant heavy cruiser Pious Fury arrives near and deploys ground forces to the human colony world of Ariel. Covenant ground forces quickly overwhelm the colonial militia and capture the majority of the planet's 4,000 colonists; despite the rapidity of their advance, however, the United Nations Space Command is alerted of the attack. The UNSC launches a small counter-attack to retake the colony, deploying two frigates and a detachment of the 105th Helljumpers to Ariel. The Covenant begins excavating shortly after securing the colony.
